UNDP GeoHub is a United Nations Development Programme (UNDP)'s cloud-based ecosystem for geospatial data, serving as a centralized platform where UNDP staff and policymakers can create, share, visualize, and analyze spatial data to support evidence-based development, particularly for achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
